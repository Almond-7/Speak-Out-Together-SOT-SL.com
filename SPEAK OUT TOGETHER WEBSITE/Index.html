Mission 

Our mission is to educate and raise awareness among young individuals regarding various societal issues. 

 
Vision 

We strive to be an effective child safeguarding organization dedicated to educating children and the broader commuinty about important societal issues. 

 
Target Groups 

Our focus is on children, youth, and parents, particurly those residing in informal settlements.  We also aim to supports survivors of sexual and gender-based violence (SGVB), sunstances misuse, and all individuals at risk of abuse.
