About Us

Speak Out Together (SOT) is a non-governmental organization established on July 25, 2024, in Sierra Leone. Our mission is to raise awareness of critical social issues, including sexual and gender-based violence, child molestation, and substance misuse. We strive to create a supportive environment for victims to share their experiences while educating the public on these issues and effective prevention strategies. Additionally, we facilitate connections between victims and relevant organizations that provide essential support and assistance.
